Who We Are

The Voice of Mogalakwena

Mokopane FM is Mogalakwena's premier community radio station, broadcasting on 100.0 MHz to the heart of Limpopo. We celebrate local culture, language, news, and music — built by the community, for the community.

From morning shows to late-night music, our programming is rooted in the stories, sounds, and spirit of Waterberg. We connect families, empower voices, and keep Mokopane informed.
