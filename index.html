Our Story

The Voice of Mogalakwena

Mokopane FM 100.0 MHz is a community-driven radio station dedicated to serving the diverse people of Mogalakwena and the Waterberg region. We believe in the power of local voices to inspire change, preserve culture, and keep our community informed.

From the latest news and traffic updates to the best in local music and cultural programming, we are your 24/7 companion. Our mission is to provide a platform for growth, education, and entertainment that resonates with the heartbeat of Mokopane.

Join us on 100.0 FM or stream us live right here on our website to stay connected to your community, no matter where you are.
